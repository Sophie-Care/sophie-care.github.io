## Introduction
Welcome to my blog. If you are reading this, you might be considering therapy but feeling a little nervous about what the process actually looks like—especially if you've had negative experiences in the past.

In this space, **neurodivergent affirming** means I don't see your brain's natural wiring as something to "fix." Instead, we work together to navigate a world that wasn't always built with you in mind.

### How we work together:
* **No "Eye Contact" Pressure:** If you prefer to look away, stim, or move while we talk, please do.
* **Creative Tools:** We can use music, art, or writing if words feel too heavy.
* **LGBTQIA+ Safe:** Your identity is celebrated, not just "tolerated."
